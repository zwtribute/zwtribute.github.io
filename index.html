A Tribute to

Zeppelin Wars

Imagine soaring through the skies, shooting down everything in your path, then jumping out onto an enemy Zeppelin, planting a bomb and watching the Zeppelin's fuel tank rupture? How about being a crew member of your Zeppelin, frantically running from spot to spot to prevent the dimise of the entire crew? Do you want to fight for or against the Pirates? THE CHOICE IS YOURS.

All of this is possible the Roblox game, Zeppelin Wars. Which is the topic of (most) of this site. Unfortunately, after just over 3 years, the game was officially discontinued. There will be no new content or any improvements to gameplay, and will thus continue to bleed players for the forseeable future.

You may be asking:

>why are you making a tribute for a dying Roblox game? That's kinda GAY bR0

That's a SWAG question. It's a really fun game. Although it's not without some quirks and issues like poor balancing and Roblox's physics engine randomly breaking, there's still a ton to like. For example: It makes great use of Roblox's part system. The game's Zeppelin destruction system wouldn't work nearly as well without every Zeppelin in the game being made of breakable parts. And there's a variety of strategies (of which many weren't intended to be utilized) that require a deep understanding of how many mechanics play out to excecute effectively. Zeppelin Wars really stands out from Roblox's crowd of low effort slop as a unique, action-packed war game with a charming, retro '00s Roblox art style.

A Brief History

The in-game news shows a healthy stream of updates right up until September 2023, where news about updates for some reason stops. Spiritual Successors

Airmada (Discord)

Airmada is pretty much going to be the premier spiritual successor of Zeppelin Wars. It intend to address all of the issues that Zeppelin Wars was plagued with (Like an ineffective team balancing system) and ZW's lag.

Battleblox (Discord)

A WIP Roblox game created by @gregheffley2235 (Discord) / @Agrostophobic (Roblox) which bears many similarities to Zeppelin Wars' art style. Although not aiming to mimic the game's Battlefield mode, it takes heavy inspiration from it. Even then, it's gameplay is intended to be slower-paced and more focused on traditional army classes (Like Medics and Infrantry) as opposed to Zeppelin Wars' simple Pilot and Crew class system. Agrostophic has finished pretty much every asset needed for the game, and has started scripting, using Zeppelin Wars: Remake/Rewrite as a base, but he plans on hiring a more skilled scripter to bring things together.

Battletriton / Battle of Attrition

A Roblox game created by L4ncer (Discord) with a semi-realistic art style. The game's lore is basically L4nce's own headcannon of the ZW universe, with a focus on world building beyond PIRATES AND POLICE KILL EACHOTHER AND DESTROY ZEPPELINS IN OHIO. It takes inspiration from both Zeppelin Wars' Battlefield mode and Centaura. Development is quite slow and seems to be quite scattered, with a bunch of random assets being made for the game which haven't even been used for anything yet, but it may eventually be released.

Development is on an indefinite hiatus, and the linked Discord is no longer dedicated to Battletrition.

Closing Thoughts

Zeppelin Wars has been open sourced as most of the development team has moved on from the game and likely would like to leave it's plethora of assets and scripts for the Roblox community to dig through and utilize.
